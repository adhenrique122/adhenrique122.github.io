The Story

A soul that refused
to kneel

In the 18th century, amid the plantations of the feared King's Farmer estate, lives Ambu — an enslaved man with a free soul and an unbreakable mind. A poet and a dreamer, he believes that true freedom does not come from broken chains, but from the power of an awakened mind.

But when strange nightmares begin to merge with reality, Ambu discovers that his spirit can cross the boundaries between the world of the living and the realm of dreams — where shadowy creatures and ancient secrets await.

"That of an Umhambi — one who can walk between dimensions and defy death itself."

As he struggles against the cruelty of his masters and the flames of a forbidden love, Ambu is forced to face his destiny. Between faith and despair, Ambu: The Dream Walker is an intense journey into the soul of a man who dared to dream of freedom.

The Author

Hélio Rizkadu

I am a visual artist and author passionate about creating dark, emotional, and immersive worlds through art and storytelling. Since I was young, I have always been fascinated by dreams, nightmares, mystery, and the unknown — and these became the foundation of my creative universe.

My work combines visual art and fiction to tell stories that go beyond words. Every illustration, scene, and character carries a deeper atmosphere inspired by fear, loneliness, imagination, and human emotion.

As both an artist and writer, I want people to feel something when they enter my world — curiosity, tension, wonder, or even discomfort. My goal is not only to create images or stories, but experiences that stay in the mind long after they are seen.

Welcome to my universe.
