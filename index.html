The Story

A soul that refused
to kneel

In the 18th century, amid the plantations of the feared King's Farmer estate, lives Ambu — an enslaved man with a free soul and an unbreakable mind. A poet and a dreamer, he believes that true freedom does not come from broken chains, but from the power of an awakened mind.

But when strange nightmares begin to merge with reality, Ambu discovers that his spirit can cross the boundaries between the world of the living and the realm of dreams — where shadowy creatures and ancient secrets await.

"That of an Umhambi — one who can walk between dimensions and defy death itself."

As he struggles against the cruelty of his masters and the flames of a forbidden love, Ambu is forced to face his destiny. Between faith and despair, Ambu: The Dream Walker is an intense journey into the soul of a man who dared to dream of freedom.

In the 18th century, amidst the plantations of the dreaded King's Farmer estate, lives Ambu, a slave with a free soul and an indomitable mind. A poet and a dreamer, he believes true liberation comes not from broken chains, but from the power of an awakened mind.

But when strange nightmares begin to blend with reality, Ambu discovers that his spirit can cross the boundaries between the world of the living and that of dreams—where dark creatures and ancient secrets await. As he battles the cruelty of the masters and the flames of a forbidden love, Ambu will be forced to face his destiny: that of an "Umhambi," one who can walk between dimensions and defy death itself.

Between faith and despair, poetry and horror, "Ambu: The Dream Walker" is an intense journey into the soul of a man who dared to dream of freedom in a world that wanted him on his knees.

Born under mysterious circumstances and raised as an enslaved child on the Silva plantation, eleven-year-old Zuri has always been haunted by strange dreams. Night after night, she witnesses the life of a man she has never met — Ambu, the poet whose spirit once challenged the boundaries between the world of the living and the realm of dreams.

But when shadowy entities from the Astral World begin hunting her, Zuri discovers that her dreams are more than memories. They are a bridge to an ancient conflict hidden for centuries.

As forgotten prophecies resurface and powerful forces move to prevent the return of a long-lost truth, Zuri must uncover the mystery of her own origins and the legacy left behind by Ambu. Guided by spirits, haunted by monsters, and pursued by the servants of the Great Dreamer, she finds herself at the center of a struggle that spans worlds.

The Author

Hélio Rizkadu

I am a visual artist and author passionate about creating dark, emotional, and immersive worlds through art and storytelling. Since I was young, I have always been fascinated by dreams, nightmares, mystery, and the unknown — and these became the foundation of my creative universe.

My work combines visual art and fiction to tell stories that go beyond words. Every illustration, scene, and character carries a deeper atmosphere inspired by fear, loneliness, imagination, and human emotion.

As both an artist and writer, I want people to feel something when they enter my world — curiosity, tension, wonder, or even discomfort. My goal is not only to create images or stories, but experiences that stay in the mind long after they are seen.

Welcome to my universe.
